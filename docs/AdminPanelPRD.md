# Product Requirement Document (PRD): Admin Panel Web Application (V1)

## 1. Product Overview
The Admin Panel is the central control center for the marketplace operations team. The V1 focus is on manual job matching, partner vetting, and overall platform oversight.

## 2. Target Audience
*   Platform administrators and operations managers.
*   Customer support and finance teams.

## 3. Key Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Booking Management & Manual Assignment
*   **Booking Overview:** Filterable list of all customer requests.
*   **Manual Matching:** Search and filter available providers by trade, location, and rating to manually assign them to a job.
*   **Assignment Override:** Ability to reassign or cancel jobs as needed.

### 3.2 Partner Management
*   **Vetting Hub:** Review and verify partner applications and documents.
*   **Status Control:** Activate, suspend, or deactivate partners.
*   **Performance Monitoring:** Overview of partner ratings and job completion rates.

### 3.3 Financial Management
*   **Transaction Logs:** Detailed view of customer payments and partner payouts.
*   **Commission Tracking:** Monitor platform revenue from service fees.
*   **Refund Management:** Process customer refund requests.

### 3.4 Platform Configuration
*   **Service Management:** Add/edit service categories, sub-services, and pricing logic.
*   **User Management:** Basic oversight of the customer database.

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements
*   **Data Density:** Optimized for desktop viewing of complex data sets.
*   **Auditability:** Logging of all critical admin actions (assignments, approvals).
*   **Security:** Role-based access control (RBAC) and secure authentication.

## 5. Success Metrics
*   **Assignment Time:** Time from booking to manual professional assignment.
*   **Vetting Throughput:** Number of partners vetted and activated per week.
*   **Platform Health:** Monitoring cancellation and refund rates.
