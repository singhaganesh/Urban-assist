# Product Requirement Document (PRD): Service Provider-Side Web Application (V1)

## 1. Product Overview
The Service Provider-Side Web Application is the professional interface for vetted partners to manage their jobs, availability, and earnings. The V1 focus is on efficient manual job acceptance and secure task execution.

## 2. Target Audience
*   Skilled home service professionals (Plumbers, Electricians, Cleaners, etc.) in the UK.
*   Sole traders and small service businesses looking for a steady stream of leads.

## 3. Key Functional Requirements

### 3.1 Onboarding & Vetting
*   **Registration:** Detailed profile creation including trade selection.
*   **Document Upload:** Secure portal for KYC (ID, Right to Work) and Trade Certifications (Insurance, DBS, Gas Safe).
*   **Status Tracking:** Real-time update on the vetting and activation progress.

### 3.2 Job Management
*   **Online/Offline Toggle:** Control availability to receive new assignments.
*   **Assignment Alerts:** Notification of jobs manually assigned by the admin.
*   **Job Details:** Access to customer address (Map view), notes, and photos upon acceptance.

### 3.3 Task Execution & Security
*   **OTP Verification:** Securely start the task by entering the customer's 4-digit code.
*   **Task Status:** Update job status to "In-Progress" and "Completed".
*   **Job Timer:** Basic tracking of task duration.

### 3.4 Financials & Performance
*   **Earnings Dashboard:** Daily, weekly, and monthly earnings overview.
*   **Payout History:** Tracking of processed and pending payments.
*   **Ratings:** View overall rating and customer feedback.

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements
*   **Reliability:** High availability for real-time job alerts.
*   **Security:** Encrypted document storage and masked communication.
*   **Mobile-First:** Optimized for use on mobile devices while on-site.

## 5. Success Metrics
*   **Acceptance Rate:** Percentage of assigned jobs accepted by providers.
*   **Onboarding Time:** Time from registration to final activation.
*   **Partner Satisfaction:** Maintaining high engagement and positive feedback.
